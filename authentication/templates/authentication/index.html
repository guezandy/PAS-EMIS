Policy Assistant System

This software is intended to strengthen data gathering and use at the various levels of the education system, which will ultimately inform policy and planning decisions. This software was developed in collaboration with the St. Lucia Education Ministry.

Data Analysis Suite

PAS has the ability to analyze historical exam scores, provide outlier analysis for schools and school districts, and track school capacity and enrollment.

School Management

School staff can enroll students, track attendance, and record grades. School supervisors have the ability to appraise teachers and principals.

Welfare Service Tracking

PAS allows school administrators to define welfare services and track which students are using these services.
